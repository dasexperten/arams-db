## Engineering Features
- **Dual-tier architecture:** Long ultra-tapered outer filaments (0.01–0.02mm) + shorter stable inner tier
- **Charcoal infusion:** Activated coconut-shell charcoal embedded into PBT filament during extrusion — no particle shedding
- **Memory spine:** Maintains bristle geometry — prevents splay after 8,000+ cycles
- **Taper geometry:** 0.01–0.02mm tips — access gingival sulcus and interproximal niches impossible for round-ended nylon
- **Hydrophobic filaments:** Charcoal-infused PBT dries faster — reduces microbial survival between brushings
