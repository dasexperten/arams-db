## Clinical Performance
- GH12 vs S. mutans: selective membrane disruption — leaves beneficial streptococci intact
- Xylitol cariostatic effect: >20 pediatric clinical trials validated
- Early childhood caries (ECC) prevention: validated probiotic + xylitol + peptide approach
- No fluoride toxicity risk — safe if full tube swallowed

## Engineering Notes
- GH12 is a synthetic antimicrobial peptide — cationic, targets negatively charged S. mutans membrane selectively
- Non-foaming formula — removes swallowing trigger associated with foam in infants
- Strawberry flavor calibrated for infant compliance without artificial sweetener overload
- Potassium sorbate + sodium benzoate dual-preservative system maintains sterility at swallow-safe doses
- Gel base (vs paste) — gentler on erupting enamel, easier parental application
