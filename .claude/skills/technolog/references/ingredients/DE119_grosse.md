## Engineering Features
- **Gold-ion (Au⁺) infusion:** Ionic gold embedded in PBT — releases Au⁺ ions on contact with saliva — bacteriostatic via enzyme inhibition in bacterial cell wall
- **Activated charcoal infusion:** Adsorbs chromogens and volatile sulfur compounds (VSC) on bristle contact
- **Wide-arc head:** ~30% larger cleaning surface — reduces brushing strokes needed for full coverage
- **High-density bristle field:** More filaments per cm² — higher contact probability per stroke

## Engineering Notes
- Au⁺ ion release mechanism: ionic gold in PBT matrix dissolves at nanomolar concentration in saliva — sufficient for bacteriostatic effect, safe for human tissue
- Activated charcoal in bristle body (not tip-coated) — sustained adsorption over full brush lifespan
- Wide arc geometry designed for users with posterior cleaning deficiency (molars)
- High-density field compensates for imprecise brushing technique
