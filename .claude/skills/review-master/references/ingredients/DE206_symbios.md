## Deep Dive — Scientific Rationale
Oral diseases such as gingivitis, halitosis, and early caries arise not only from bacterial overgrowth but from **microbiome imbalance**. Traditional antibacterial dentifrices rely on chemical suppression — chlorhexidine, triclosan, broad-spectrum antiseptics — which reduce both pathogenic and beneficial species, destabilizing oral ecology and risking rebound dysbiosis.

SYMBIOS introduces a fundamentally different model: a **microbiome-restorative formulation** using Bacillus coagulans, a clinically studied probiotic capable of:
- Modulating oral flora
- Inhibiting pathogenic species
- Suppressing inflammatory pathways

The formulation aims to shift oral health from pathogen killing toward **ecological stability**, aligning with modern microbiome science.

With Bacillus coagulans as its active microbiome regulator, SYMBIOS delivers a scientifically justified, stable, and effective probiotic solution aligned with the future of preventive oral health.

## Engineering Notes
- Bacillus coagulans is spore-forming — survives toothpaste manufacturing, shelf storage, and stomach acid
- Spore activation occurs at oral temperature (37°C) and salivary pH (6.8–7.4)
- Xanthan gum maintains uniform probiotic suspension throughout product shelf life
- No broad-spectrum antiseptics used — preserves beneficial oral flora
- LS 30 surfactant chosen specifically to avoid denaturing probiotic cultures (unlike SLS)
- Sodium pyrophosphate operates as anti-tartar agent without interfering with probiotic viability
