- **Filament Diameter:** 0.15–0.18mm — soft/medium hybrid profile
- **Tip Geometry:** Rounded PBT tips — gum-safe, no micro-laceration risk
- **Bristle Field:** Multi-height arrangement — outer short perimeter + inner medium + central tall cluster
- **Handle:** Anti-slip micro-texture grip — TPR overlay on PP core
