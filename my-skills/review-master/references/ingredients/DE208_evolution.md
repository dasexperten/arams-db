## Engineering Notes
- CPP-ACP acts as a mineral reservoir — releases Ca²⁺ and PO₄³⁻ ions at low pH (acid challenge) and reabsorbs at neutral pH
- Recaldent™ is the patented form of CPP-ACP — derived from milk casein (note: milk protein allergy contraindication)
- B. coagulans survives manufacturing and storage as spore — activates at oral temperature
- Child-safe abrasivity (silica calibrated to <50 RDA) — safe for erupting and mixed dentition
- SLS-free eliminates aphthous ulcer trigger common in children
