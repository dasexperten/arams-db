# DE112 — EXPANDING Floss — Ingredient File

## Product Identity
- **Article:** DE112
- **Format:** Dental floss — expanding polyester
- **Category:** Hydrophilic Multifilament Blooming Floss

## Materials Composition
| Component | Material | Specification | Function |
|---|---|---|---|
| Filament | Hydrophilic polyester multifilament | Blooms 2–3× width on contact with saliva/water | Mixed contacts, restorations, wide embrasures |
| Coating | None (uncoated) | Pure hydrophilic surface | Maximum expansion, water absorption |

## Engineering Features
- **Hydrophilic multifilament:** Individual microfilaments swell on moisture contact — total width expands 2–3× pre-use diameter
- **Bloom mechanism:** Capillary absorption of saliva triggers filament separation — contact surface area multiplies
- **Adaptation:** Expanded width conforms to irregular interproximal surface — contacts restoration margins, implant surfaces, wide embrasures
- **Shredding resistance:** Multifilament twist construction — individual filament failure does not compromise overall floss integrity

## Key Performance Data
- Expansion ratio: 2–3× diameter on saliva contact
- Contact surface increase: proportional to expansion — 4–9× original contact area
- Plaque removal: enhanced vs monofilament due to expanded surface contact
- Shredding risk: low (multifilament redundancy)

## Key Flags
- ✅ Mixed restoration compatible
- ✅ Implant safe
- ✅ Wide embrasure compatible
- ✅ Orthodontic safe

## Engineering Notes
- Hydrophilic polyester: surface energy modification promotes water uptake — bloom begins within 2–3 seconds of interproximal placement
- Multifilament twist: 12–24 individual microfilaments — twist angle controls bloom rate and final expanded geometry
