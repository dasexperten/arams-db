| Component | Material | Specification | Function |
|---|---|---|---|
| Filament | Polyester monofilament/multifilament | Standard gauge | Base floss structure |

## Engineering Features
- **Wax coating:** Reduces interproximal friction coefficient — smooth entry in tight contacts, implant thread grooves, retainer wire zones
- **Peppermint extract in wax:** Dual function — flavor + mild antimicrobial (menthol) on contact with interproximal surfaces
- **Low-friction surface:** Prevents shredding in tight contacts, snap-through trauma in tight embrasures

## Key Performance Data
- Primary target: tight contacts, implants, retainers
- Friction reduction: wax coating reduces insertion force by 30–40%
- Shredding resistance: wax-bonded filaments — coherent entry in tight spaces
- Freshness: peppermint sustained release during flossing

## Key Flags
- ✅ Implant-safe

## Engineering Notes
- Microcrystalline wax: higher melting point than paraffin — maintains coating integrity at oral temperature (37°C)
- Peppermint in wax matrix: sustained menthol release as wax fraction is removed during interproximal movement — not a single-burst flavor release
