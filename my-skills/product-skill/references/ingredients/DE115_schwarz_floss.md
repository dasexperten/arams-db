## Product Identity
- **Article:** DE115
- **Format:** Dental floss — charcoal expanding
- **Category:** Bamboo + Coconut Charcoal Expanding Floss — Visual Plaque Detection

## Materials Composition
| Component | Material | Specification | Function |
|---|---|---|---|
| Filament | Hydrophilic multifilament polyester | Expanding on moisture contact | Interproximal surface contact |
| Charcoal Infusion | Bamboo charcoal + coconut-shell activated charcoal | Integrated into filament body | Chromogen adsorption, visual plaque detection, deodorizing |

## Engineering Features
- **Dual-charcoal infusion:** Bamboo charcoal (high surface area, soft) + coconut-shell activated charcoal (microporous, high adsorption capacity) — embedded in filament, not surface-coated
- **Visual plaque detection:** Black filament shows white/yellow plaque deposits on contact — user feedback on cleaning completeness
- **Expanding mechanism:** Hydrophilic multifilament blooms on saliva contact — same expansion mechanics as DE112
- **Chromogen adsorption:** Charcoal contacts chromogens and VSC molecules interproximally — deodorizing effect

## Key Performance Data
- Visual feedback: plaque contrast visible against black filament — 100% user-detectable
- Adsorption: activated charcoal surface area >500m²/g — high chromogen capture capacity
- Expansion: 2–3× bloom on saliva contact
- Deodorizing: VSC (volatile sulfur compound) adsorption confirmed for activated charcoal
